Task definitions
SM Surgical Migration

When CSP tickets remain unresolved for more than 72 hours, the affected customer (CX) must be migrated to a nearby overlapping CSP.

EM Exit Migration

The entire U1 customer base (CX) of a CSP exiting Wiom must be migrated to a nearby overlapping CSP.

DP Device Pickups

The entire U2 customer base of a CSP exiting Wiom requires device collection from customers' homes.

TL TrustLine

Any technical issue that the PTL team is unable to resolve will be escalated to the NQT team through the TrustLine Slack channel.

CV CSP Visit

If a TrustLine technical issue cannot be resolved remotely, the team must visit the CSP office after scheduling an appointment.

NV NTT Visit

Required for network monitoring, network assessment, and network design activities in coordination with the NTT team.
